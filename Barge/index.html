Our Story

Every Fla vor Tells a Story

The Barge Pub in Mysore is a vibrant microbrewery and pub offering a unique blend of freshly brewed craft beers and a lively atmosphere. Known for its exceptional brews and diverse food menu, it attracts both locals and visitors. The pub stands out for its special weekend events, including karaoke nights and DJ performances, which create a dynamic and engaging nightlife experience. Whether you’re in the mood to sing your heart out, dance to the latest beats, or simply enjoy a great drink in a fun setting, The Barge Pub delivers an entertaining and memorable experience.
